# HEHA Vendor Pricing Policy

## Current decision

For local vendor and cottage-food style products, HEHA should keep product prices at the vendor's original listed price.

HEHA revenue should be collected as a separate customer-facing delivery/service fee, not as an embedded markup inside each product price.

## Orawganic setup

- Partner type: local food vendor
- Product price policy: vendor original price, no HEHA markup
- HEHA fee model: separate customer delivery/service fee
- Fee amount: $17.50
- Delivery days: Monday-Friday only
- Customer-facing wording: Vendor price plus $17.50 HEHA delivery/service fee

## Reason

This keeps the customer experience transparent, protects partner trust, and avoids making HEHA look like it is taking a per-item vendor commission.
